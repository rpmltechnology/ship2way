Order Management System

Ship2way’s order management software helps you organize your ecommerce orders easily and effectively. With Ship2way we provide several ways to manage all of your online orders and automate many tasks associated with order fulfillment.

Control all online store from one dashboard

When there are multiple orders for the same shipping address, Ship2way will alert a user and have them combine the order into a single Ship2way and shipping label while maintaining the integrity of each order.

Upload Download from and to many online store at the same time

When shipping a single order with items from different locations, Ship2way allows you to split orders into multiple Ship2ways.when an item has reached its product threshold, and much more.

Ship your order to many Carrier from any where

Filters allow you to view orders that match only your selected criteria, like destination, tag, store, and many others. Combine filters for a powerful way to quickly view and prioritize orders or flag orders that need special attention.

Able to moniter your order from many online store through one dashboard

Ship2way has pre-built alerts to notify a user when an action needs to be taken. Order alerts let you know if you need to combine orders, when an item has reached its product threshold, and much more.

7 days free trial

Order Management Reporting

With Ship2way you can quickly view, filter, and export the data you need for orders, individually or grouped. Each report has various filter options to help you export only the data you need, whether the data is within a specific date range or from a specific store or carrier account.

Order Reports

The order reports pulls data specific to synced orders that have been either imported, or created in Ship2way.

Product Reports

The products report pulls data specific to the products included on order records in Ship2way or products included on orders with returns.

Ship2way Reports

The Ship2ways report pulls data specific to Ship2way records in Ship2way. These reports do not include fulfillments, orders marked as shipped, or orders updated to the “Shipped” status via store updates.

Edit Orders

There are times when order details change, which is why Ship2way allows you to edit order details even after the import has taken place. These details include address, payment, item details, and order notes.
